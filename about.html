ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Jamshed. I’m originally from Tajikistan and have been living in Portugal for the past three and a half years. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in International Relations, which shaped my ability to think critically, research deeply, and communicate clearly.

After graduation, I spent several years working in hospitality as a bartender. It might sound far from data, but that experience taught me how to adapt quickly, work under pressure, and connect with people from all walks of life — skills that now help me approach data problems with both structure and empathy.

My journey into data truly began when I decided to fully immerse myself through Le Wagon’s intensive two-month Data Analytics Bootcamp in Lisbon. There, I developed hands-on skills in Python, SQL, data visualization, and building analytical dashboards, and discovered how much I enjoy transforming raw data into meaningful insights.

Right now, I’m working while actively building data projects, offering freelancing services, and open to full-time opportunities as a Junior Data Analyst. My goal is to bring my diverse background and technical skills together to help organizations make smarter, data-driven decisions.
